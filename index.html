My cleaners don't use apps.
Gleam works on any smartphone. It opens in a browser — no download required. If your cleaner can send a WhatsApp message, they can use Gleam.
I already use WhatsApp for this.
WhatsApp tells you what your cleaner says they did. Gleam shows you what they actually did — with a timestamped photo for every item, every room, every job.
I don't have time to set this up.
Most owners have their first property and first job scheduled within 20 minutes of signing up. The setup is fast because we built it for people who aren't into software.
What if my cleaner forgets to use it?
They see their jobs the moment they open the app. The checklist is their workflow — there's nothing to remember. And you see immediately if a job hasn't been started.

Each company on Gleam sees only their own data. No other business can access your properties, your clients, or your jobs. Ever.
